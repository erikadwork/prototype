Smiskis



One popular blind box series would be the Smiskis' blind box series. This series features intriguing glow in the dark human figures in different poses or activities. These adorable yet odd figurines can be put on display for aesthetics and enjoyment or even as toys for the younger crowd. The concept of a blind box adds an element of surprise to each purchase, making unboxing an exciting adventure. The packaging is thoughtfully designed as it is a hexagon-looking box with bright colors. Each box promises the thrill of discovery, as buyers have no idea which variation of the striking green figures they will receive—making each unboxing an unforgettable experience that combines the joy of surprise with the allure of art.

In terms of design, these green human figures push the limits of traditional collectibles. While other collectibles have more intricate designs, these figures favor simplicity, featuring a basic, emotionless face. Their vibrant green color creates a unique aesthetic, enhanced by their glow-in-the-dark feature. Their minimalistic design encourages viewers to appreciate their actions and poses rather than getting lost in intricate detail.

Despite the enjoyable aspects, the blind box format does have drawbacks. The unpredictability may lead to duplicate figures, frustrating collectors who want to complete a full set. Additionally, some production inconsistencies have been reported, and the pricing can be high given the potential for duplicates.

Overall, the blind box Smiskis green human figures provide an innovative collectible experience that blends art with the thrill of mystery. Their simple yet striking design appeals to those who value originality and the excitement of surprise.

PalVerse: Haikyu!!



The "PalVerse:Haikyu!!" series brings an electrifying twist to the world of collectible figures by transforming the high-octane energy of the hit volleyball anime into an adorable, chibi-inspired blind box format. Each mini figure is crafted to capture the character’s unique personality in a roughly 3½‑inch tall form. The element of surprise—never knowing which character you’ll receive until you open the box—adds extra excitement to the unboxing experience.

At its core, the series celebrates the broader PalVerse concept, a sub-brand from Bushiroad Creative that reimagines beloved characters in a “new world in your palm.” With a signature deformed, chibi aesthetic, PalVerse transforms iconic characters into vibrant, toy-like renditions that feel as though they’ve leaped off a painter’s palette. Specifically, this series is a collaboration of the popular volleyball show "Haikyuu!". They include some fan favorite characters such as "Hinata Shoyo", "Kageyama Tobio", "Tsukishima Kei" and a few others!

Collectors love the attention to detail—from dynamic poses and expressive faces to the carefully designed packaging that enhances each unboxing. While the blind box format can lead to duplicates, it also heightens the anticipation and thrill of collecting. Whether completing a full set or enjoying the surprise with every box, the “PalVerse:Haikyu!!” series stands out as a perfect blend of artistry, fun, and fandom.

In short, PalVerse is a celebration of creativity and design transformation in the collectible world—a must-have for fans of Haikyu!! and beyond.
